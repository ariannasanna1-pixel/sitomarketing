About us

Our team.

Six design students with 300 screenshots, 20 open tabs, and zero organization. Every year was the same. Until we decided to do something about it.

Our
manifesto
"Too many things to see and no truly simple way to experience them all."
Every year at Design Week, we say the same things. We miss exhibitions because we didn't write them down. We find a screenshot of a showroom three weeks after it closed. We arrive late because we didn't know there was a queue.

We are six design students. We know the problem better than anyone else because we were the problem.

So we stopped complaining. We built the tool we always wanted to have — simple, fast, global. One single place for everything happening during Design Week, in every city in the world.
